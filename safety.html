Safety Information

At Shuttle, safety is our top priority. We understand that space travel can be a daunting prospect, which is why we have taken every measure to ensure the safety of our passengers. Our spaceships are designed and built with the latest technology and materials, ensuring that they are able to withstand the rigors of space travel.

At Shuttle Space Travel, we use proprietary oxygen regulators on our spacecraft to ensure a safe and efficient journey. Our in-house designed regulators provide a steady flow of oxygen, eliminating the risk of deprivation and ensuring a comfortable trip. They are also more reliable and easy to maintain, making them an industry leader in terms of safety and efficiency.

At Shuttle Space Travel, we take customer safety extremely seriously. That's why our spacecrafts are equipped with triple-layered radiation shields. These shields provide an added layer of protection against harmful radiation, which can be present during space travel. The shields are made of advanced polymers that are able to block and absorb the radiation, keeping our passengers safe at all times.
